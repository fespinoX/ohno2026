About_me

Hey! I'm Fespi, a UI/UX Product Designer based in Buenos Aires, Argentina. I think great design is a lot like a good game: intuitive, engaging, and satisfying to use.

I have 15+ years of experience designing digital products for companies around the world, across both B2B and B2C platforms. I enjoy turning complex ideas into simple, usable products.

When I start a project, the first question I ask is who are our users? Understanding how people interact with a product guides my decisions.

I started as a frontend developer, which helps me bridge the gap between design and implementation. I see design as a team sport and enjoy working closely with developers and product teams.

You can say I’m a big design nerd, inspired by art, movies, video games, and board games.
