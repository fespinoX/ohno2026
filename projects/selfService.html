Self-Service

I’ve worked on several self-service platforms designed to help users solve problems on their own without needing to rely on support or manual processes. The focus is always on making complex workflows clear and intuitive, so people can quickly find what they need, complete tasks confidently, and stay in control of their experience.

// Project Details

This platform lets creators power their AI assistant by connecting their social media accounts and managing the content the AI learns from. I designed the user panel where creators configure access to their social platforms, upload or edit content in their knowledge base, and control how their AI “Goldie” behaves. The goal was to make it easy for non-technical users to manage their data, keep their content up to date, and personalize the experience without needing support.
